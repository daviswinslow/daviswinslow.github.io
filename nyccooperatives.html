• New York City Worker Cooperatives
  • About
    • This is a map of worker owned cooperatives in New York City, produced as a project for a class in advanced geographic information systems at The New School. The data used for this map was derived from the New York City Network of Worker Cooperatives. Clicking on an icon will bring up the business name in the info table on the right, and will soon include more detailed information. The business directory and search filters are under development, as is the feature markers.
